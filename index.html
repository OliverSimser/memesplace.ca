About Us

We are an Infants and Toddlers home daycare. Growing up independent and unique, fostering self respect, creativity, curiosity, and life appreciation in a personal and nurturing environment.

- Elementary School Teacher with (+) 15 years of experience as an Early Childhood Educator
- Outdoor activities and arts stimulation. English & first steps in French.
- Homemade food, ingredients from local and organic producers.
- Flexible hours
- Daily reports
- First Aid CPR Cert and Police record checked
- Low ratio: ONLY 4 FULL TIME SPOTS (from 8 months to 4 years old) to offer better care and attention.
- Outdoors and indoors spaces for gross & fine motor skills, music instruments, playroom promoting discovery and creativity. One bedroom for each child.
Having fun surrounded by nature to develop life appreciation
